Run Smooth: Generator Care & Safety

● Professional Exhaust Routing

Never install a generator indoors. Carbon Monoxide is odorless and deadly. Our pros ensure the exhaust faces away from windows and vents.

● Automatic Changeover Safety

Incorrect manual changeover wiring can "backfeed" power into the grid, endangering utility workers. We recommend automated panels for seamless, safe transitions.

● The 50-Hour Oil Rule

For small and medium petrol units, change your oil every 50 operating hours. Fresh oil prevents engine "knocking" and expensive piston repairs.

● Stable Foundation Placement

Vibration is the enemy of your alternator. Install your generator on a level, concrete pad to prevent internal components from shaking loose over time.
