Extend Your Power: Battery Health Tips

● Avoid Deep Discharge

Try not to let your system drop below 30% charge. Frequent "deep cycling" can permanently reduce the capacity of AGM and Tubular batteries.

● Keep it Cool & Ventilated

Ensure your inverter setup is in a well-ventilated area. Excessive heat during charging can cause battery plates to warp or casings to swell.

● Use Only Distilled Water

For Tubular batteries, only top up with distilled water. Using tap water introduces minerals that create chemical imbalances and destroy cells.

● Terminal Maintenance

Watch for white or blue crust (corrosion) on terminals. Keeping these clean ensures a steady, safe flow of power to your home.
