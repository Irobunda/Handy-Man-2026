Brighter Living: Lighting & Electrical Safety

● Match Your Wattage

Always check the maximum wattage rating of your fixture. Using a bulb that exceeds this limit can overheat the socket and create a fire hazard.

● LED Energy Efficiency

Switching to LED bulbs reduces heat output and cuts lighting energy costs by up to 80% compared to traditional incandescent bulbs.

● Weatherproof Outdoor Zones

Outdoor lights must have an IP65 rating or higher. This prevents rain from entering the housing and tripping your circuit breaker.

● Reinforced Chandelier Mounting

Heavy fixtures require a reinforced junction box. Our pros ensure your ceiling can support the weight before the final installation begins.
