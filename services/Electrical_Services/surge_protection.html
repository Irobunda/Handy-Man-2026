Safeguard Your Home: Surge Protection & Earthing

● The "First Line" Defense

A Whole-House Surge Protector (Type 2 SPD) installed at your main DB box stops high-voltage spikes from the grid before they reach your internal sockets.

● Grounding is Mandatory

Surge protectors work by "shunting" excess electricity into the ground. If your home's earthing is weak or non-existent, the surge protector cannot protect your devices.

● Protection for Sensitive Boards

Modern Inverter ACs and Smart TVs have delicate motherboards. Standard stabilizers often react too slowly; an SPD provides the nanosecond response time needed.

● Monitor Your SPD Status

Most surge protectors have a green "Protection On" light. If this light turns red or goes out, the device has sacrificed itself to stop a surge and must be replaced immediately.
