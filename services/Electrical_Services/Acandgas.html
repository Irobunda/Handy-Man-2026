Stay Cool: Professional AC Maintenance

● Clean Filters Bi-Weekly

In dusty environments, clogged filters force the compressor to work 30% harder, leading to higher electricity bills and eventual system failure.

● The "Ice" Warning

If you see ice forming on the copper pipes of your outdoor unit, turn it off immediately. This typically indicates a refrigerant (gas) leak or restricted airflow.

● Clear the Outdoor Condenser

Ensure your outdoor unit has at least 2 feet of space. Obstructions prevent heat release, causing the system to overheat and shut down.

● Annual Chemical Wash

A professional chemical wash clears internal coils you cannot reach, removing deep-seated grime and significantly extending the unit's lifespan.
