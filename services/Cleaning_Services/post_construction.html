The Heavy-Duty Difference: Industrial Standards

● Solvent-Based Stain Removal

Construction debris like cement grout and paint splatters require more than soap. Our pros use specialized solvents that safely lift tough stains without etching or damaging your vitrified tiles or marble.

● Industrial Dust Extraction

Construction dust is microscopic and settles everywhere. We use industrial-grade HEPA vacuums that capture 99.9% of particles from walls, ceilings, and inside cabinets, ensuring a truly breathable home.

● Window Track Detailing

New buildings often have sand and grit trapped in window sliders. We don't just clean the glass; we deep-scrub the tracks to ensure smooth operation and prevent long-term hardware damage.

● Interior Fixture Sanitization

For move-in services, we focus on the "hidden" areas. We sanitize the insides of every closet, kitchen drawer, and bathroom vanity so you can unpack your belongings immediately with total peace of mind.
