A Sparkle You Can Trust: Pro Cleaning Tips

● High-Touch Point Sanitization

Standard cleaning isn't just about floors. Our pros focus on "high-touch" areas like doorknobs, light switches, and remote controls to eliminate bacteria and viruses.

● Cross-Contamination Prevention

We use a color-coded microfiber system. This ensures that the cloth used to clean the bathroom never touches your kitchen surfaces, maintaining the highest hygiene standards.

● Dusting from Top to Bottom

The professional way to clean is gravity-led. We start with ceiling fans and high shelves so that any falling dust is captured when we finally vacuum and mop the floors.

● Eco-Friendly Solutions

Hard chemicals can damage your lungs and surfaces. Upon request, our pros can use non-toxic, pet-safe cleaning agents that leave your home smelling fresh without the fumes.
