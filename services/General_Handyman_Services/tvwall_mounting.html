Viewing Perfection: Pro Mounting Tips

● Ideal Viewing Height

The center of your TV should be at eye-level when seated (usually 42 inches from the floor). Mounting too high causes neck strain, often called "TV-over-fireplace" syndrome.

● Securing to Concrete vs. Studs

In Nigeria, most walls are solid blocks requiring Fischer plugs. For modern plasterboard walls, we find the wooden "studs" to ensure the TV doesn't rip out of the drywall.

● Cable Concealment & Heat

Concealing cables inside a wall looks great but requires proper routing to avoid heat buildup. We ensure your HDMI and power cables are spaced to maintain peak performance.

● The "Level" Guarantee

Even a 1-degree tilt is visible on a 75" screen. Our pros use professional spirit levels and laser guides to ensure your TV is perfectly horizontal every time.
