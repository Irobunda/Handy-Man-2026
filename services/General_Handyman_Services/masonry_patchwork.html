Seamless Repairs: Pro Masonry Tips

● The "Bonding" Secret

Plastering over old cement often leads to cracks. Our pros use a bonding agent (SBR) to ensure the new mortar sticks permanently to the old wall, preventing that annoying "hollow" sound later.

● Preventing Shrinkage Cracks

Applying thick plaster all at once leads to cracking as it dries. We apply masonry patches in layers—allowing the "scratch coat" to set before finishing with a smooth screeding putty.

● Matching Tile Grout

When replacing a single tile, the grout color is the giveaway. We don't just use "white" or "grey"—we mix pigments to match the aged grout in the rest of your room for a truly invisible repair.

● Dust Containment

Masonry is dusty. We use wet-cutting techniques for tiles and plastic screening for wall repairs to ensure that cement dust doesn't settle on your electronics or furniture.
