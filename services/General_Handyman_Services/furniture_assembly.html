Precision Building: Pro Assembly Tips

● Squaring the Frame

A wardrobe that isn't "square" will have doors that don't close right. We use corner levels to ensure the frame is 100% vertical before the back panel is nailed on.

● Drawer Runner Alignment

The #1 fail in DIY assembly is backward or uneven drawer runners. Our pros identify the "Left" and "Right" tracks immediately, ensuring a smooth, soft-close glide every time.

● Cam-Lock Security

Over-tightening or under-tightening cam-locks causes flat-pack furniture to wobble or collapse. We use precision torque to lock panels together permanently without stripping the wood.

● Child-Safety Wall Anchoring

In homes with children, tall dressers and wardrobes are tipping hazards. We provide and install anti-tip brackets to secure your furniture to the wall for total peace of mind.
