The Perfect Hang: Pro Window Tips

● Steps for a Perfect Fit

    Width: Measure the top, middle, and bottom of the window frame. Use the widest for curtains and the narrowest for inside-mount blinds.

    Drop (Height): Measure from your desired rail position down to the end point (sill or floor).

    Clearance: Ensure at least 2 inches of flat wall space above the window for the concrete lintel brackets.

● "High and Wide" Rule

To make your room feel taller, we install rods 4-6 inches above the frame and extend them 3-6 inches beyond the sides. This creates a grand, luxurious aesthetic.

● Dealing with Concrete Lintels

Nigerian windows often have reinforced concrete beams above them. We use SDS rotary hammer drills to ensure a deep, secure anchor without cracking your plaster.

● Bracket Spacing for Heavy Fabrics

Heavy blackout curtains can weigh over 10kg. We install center support brackets for any span over 4 feet to prevent the rod from bowing or pulling out.

● Blind Safety & Tension

For Zebra blinds, we mount tension pulleys securely. This prevents tangling and ensures the blind rolls up straight every time.
