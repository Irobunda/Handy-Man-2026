Woodcraft Excellence: Pro Maintenance Tips

● Seasonal Swelling & Shrinkage

Nigeria's humidity causes wood to expand in the rainy season and shrink during the Harmattan. Our pros don't just shave jammed doors; we seal raw edges with moisture-resistant lacquer to prevent future warping.

● Anti-Termite Structural Treatment

Pests can ruin a roof framework in months. We apply industrial-grade anti-termite treatment to all rafters, noggings, and skirting boards during repairs to ensure your structural wood stays solid for decades.

● Hinge & Runner Reinforcement

Kitchen and wardrobe doors often fail at the hinges. We use heavy-duty hydraulic hinges and soft-close runners that distribute weight better, preventing the "sagging" common in HDF and MDF furniture.

● Roof Framework Integrity

A small roof leak can rot your entire ceiling frame. During repairs, we inspect the load-bearing timber for soft spots, replacing weakened sections before they lead to a dangerous ceiling collapse.
