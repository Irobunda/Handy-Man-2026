Artisan Excellence: Pro Furniture Tips

● Identifying Structural Fatigue

A "wobbly" chair is often caused by glue failure in the dowel joints. We don't just add screws; we disassemble, clean out old glue, and re-clamp the joints with industrial-strength resin.

● The Power of Refinishing

Solid wood can be sanded down and re-stained up to 5 times. We can change your old dark mahogany look to a modern light oak or "naked wood" finish without you buying new furniture.

● Upcycling & Sustainability

Recycling furniture isn't just eco-friendly; it's cost-effective. An old solid wood bed frame can be repurposed into a high-end TV console or entryway bench with our custom redesign service.

● Dealing with Furniture Borers

Small holes and "wood dust" indicate active pests. During repair, we inject anti-borer chemicals into the wood grain to kill larvae and seal the wood to prevent future infestations.
