Structural Integrity: The Handy Standard

● Rafter Spacing & Load Distribution

Proper roofing requires precise rafter spacing (usually 600mm) to support the weight of stone-coated tiles or long-span aluminum. We ensure weight is distributed to the load-bearing walls, not the ceiling.

● Anti-Termite Immersion

Structural wood in Nigeria faces aggressive pest threats. Our pros apply high-concentration anti-termite chemicals to every joint and beam before installation to prevent internal wood-rot.

● The Danger of "Soggy" Noggings

If your ceiling looks like it's sagging, the wooden noggings are likely compromised by water. We replace water-logged wood with treated timber to prevent P.O.P collapse.

● Storm Bracing

During the rainy season, wind uplift can pull a roof off. We install additional "hurricane ties" and cross-bracing to secure your trusses to the concrete ring beam of the house.
