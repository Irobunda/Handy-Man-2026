Craftsmanship Excellence: Pro Installation Tips

● Seasonal Swelling & Shrinkage

Nigeria's humidity causes wood to expand and shrink between seasons. Our pros don't just fit doors; we leave precise "expansion gaps" and seal raw edges to prevent warping.

● Anti-Termite Structural Treatment

Pests can ruin expensive cabinetry in months. We apply industrial-grade treatment to the back-panels and base of all wardrobes and kitchen units during installation.

● Hinge & Runner Reinforcement

Kitchen and wardrobe doors often fail at the hinges. We use heavy-duty hydraulic hinges and soft-close runners that distribute weight better, preventing the "sagging" common in local builds.

● Load-Bearing Integrity

A heavy kitchen island or overhead cabinet needs secure anchoring. We use reinforced wall plugs and bracket systems to ensure your storage stays safely in place.
