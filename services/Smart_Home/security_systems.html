Secure Your Boundary: Professional Fence Care

● The Importance of Earthing

An electric fence is only effective if the intruder completes the circuit to the ground. Our pros ensure your earth spikes are deep and corrosion-free for maximum shock delivery.

● Vegetation Control

Branches touching your wires cause "arcing," which drains your battery and creates false alarms. Regular clearing ensures your fence stays at full voltage (typically 7,000V - 9,000V).

● Wire Tensioning

Sagging wires are easy to bypass. We use specialized tensioners to keep wires taut, ensuring that any attempt to spread the wires triggers the alarm immediately.

● Battery Health Checks

Your fence must work even during a power outage. We test your 12V backup battery to ensure it can power the energizer for at least 8-12 hours during an "NEPA" blackout.
