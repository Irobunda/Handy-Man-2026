Seamless Connectivity: Professional WiFi Tips

● Centralize Your Router

WiFi signals travel in spheres. Placing your router in a corner or inside a cabinet wastes half the signal. Our pros find the central "line-of-sight" point to maximize coverage.

● Channel Optimization

If your neighbors have routers on the same frequency, your speed will drop due to interference. we scan the airwaves and switch your router to the least crowded channel.

● Mesh vs. Extenders

Standard extenders often cut your speed in half. For large homes, we recommend Mesh systems that create one seamless, high-speed network throughout every floor.

● Wired Backhaul for Gaming

For serious gaming or 4K streaming, wireless isn't enough. We can install hidden Ethernet cabling to provide a 100% stable, lag-free connection where you need it most.
