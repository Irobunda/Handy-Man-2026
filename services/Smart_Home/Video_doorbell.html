Smart Entry: Professional Doorbell Setup

● Optimal Mounting Height

We recommend mounting your doorbell at 4 feet (48 inches) from the ground. This height is perfect for capturing faces clearly while ensuring the motion sensors detect approaching visitors effectively.

● WiFi Strength Optimization

Metal doors and thick walls can kill your WiFi signal. Our pros test your signal strength at the door and can recommend or install range extenders to prevent "laggy" video.

● Voltage & Transformer Checks

Wired doorbells require specific voltage (usually 16V-24V). Using an old transformer can fry your new device. We test your wires before connecting to ensure a safe power supply.

● Privacy Zone Configuration

Respect your neighbors. We help you set up digital "Privacy Zones" to block out neighboring properties from your recording while keeping your own entrance fully secure.
