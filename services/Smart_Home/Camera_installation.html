Secure Your Home: Professional CCTV Setup

● Strategic Blind Spot Coverage

A professional doesn't just hang cameras; they map your property to eliminate "blind spots." High-entry points and dark corners are prioritized for maximum deterrence.

● Night Vision & Lighting

Infrared (IR) range is critical. We ensure your cameras are positioned so that wall reflections don't "blind" the sensor at night, keeping your footage crisp 24/7.

● Secure Remote Viewing

Don't leave your security system open to hackers. Our pros set up secure, encrypted mobile viewing so you can check your home from anywhere in the world safely.

● Cable Protection

Exposed cables are easily cut by intruders. We use conduits and junction boxes to hide all wiring, ensuring your system stays online when you need it most.
