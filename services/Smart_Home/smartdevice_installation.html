Smart Living: Professional Device Setup

● Ecosystem Compatibility

Not all devices play well together. Our pros ensure your new gadget is fully compatible with your existing hub (Alexa, Google, or Apple) for seamless voice control.

● Secure WiFi Configuration

Smart devices are common entry points for hackers. We set up your devices on a secure, isolated network layer to keep your personal data and home privacy protected.

● Neutral Wire Verification

Most smart switches require a "Neutral" wire to function. Before we start, our pros verify your home's wiring to ensure a safe and compatible installation.

● Automation & Scene Setup

Don't just turn it on; automate it. We can help you set up "Scenes"—like automatically turning off lights and locking doors when you leave the house.
